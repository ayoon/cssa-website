Michelle Huang -- President

Salutations! My name is Michelle and I’m so excited to be your president this year. I’m a graduating senior in Cognitive Science major. First and foremost, I love to make puns and jokes, like really REALLY bad ones. In addition to exploring the infinite mysteries of the mind, I am also interested in the field of public health, specifically in mental health services. I’ve also had experience researching in labs at Cal. I’ve worked in the Sleep and Cognition Lab under Matt Walker working on data analysis and running experiments (mainly with EEG) to look at the effect that sleep has on cognitive performance and emotion.
